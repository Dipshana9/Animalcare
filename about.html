Our mission is to make all animals feel safe and cared for.

Pawfert Pals is a non profit organization started with a belief that no pets should be left behind and each of them deserve a home.

Read our story

Our founding

With the increase in street animals being the victims of accidents and having no proper vaccination, a team of 5 students decided it was time to commit the change and start from themselves. The change first started from a small rebetd room to the building it is now. The words started spreading and soon it became a home for hundreds of animals, some got the pals they wanted from there.

Our vision

A society where all animals are treated with kindness, respect, and dignity, and no animal suffers due to neglect or cruelty.
